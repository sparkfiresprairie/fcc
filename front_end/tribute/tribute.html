Dr. Deng JiaXian

Father of China's Nuclear Program

Deng Jiaxian in Aug. 1950, apparently after receiving his Ph.D.

Here's a time line of Dr. Deng's life:

  • 1924 - He was born on June 25, 1924, in Huaining, Anhui Province
  • 1950 - He completed his PhD in physics at Purdue University
  • 1950 - Just 9 days after graduation, he decided to return to China to contribute to the scientific development of the newly founded People's Republic of China
  • 1958 - He started working secretly with a team of young scientists on the development of the nuclear and hydrogen bomb for China
  • 1964 - Nuclear bomb was successfully created
  • 1967 - Hydrogen bomb was successfully created
  • 1986 - He died on July 29, 1986, in Beijing
  • 1999 - He was posthumously awarded the National Merit Medal for his contributions to Chinese military science
